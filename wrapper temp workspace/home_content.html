Omega-3: How eating fish helps your heart.
The omega-3 fatty acids in fish are good for your heart. If you're worried about heart disease  whether you want to avoid it, or you already have it and want to get healthier  eating one to two servings of fish a week could reduce your risk of dying of a heart attack by a third or more.
